Connect with those Who Care

Support Finder Tutorial

  1. Navigate to the 'Find' tab and select your struggle tags, emphasizing "depression".

  2. Set filters for gender, age range, and location.

  3. Tap 'Find' to display people nearby who share similar struggles.

  4. Select a profile that resonates with you and tap 'Connect'.

Swift Assistance at Your Fingertips

Emergency Contact Feature

  1. Tap the red button to add an emergency contact.

  2. Connect with the user you want as your emergency contact.

  3. Send a request to confirm them as your emergency contact.

  4. Tap the red button to call for immediate support when needed.

Empower Your Journey with Progress Tracking

Progress Path Tracker Tutorial

  1. Create a habit to track: positive or negative.

    • Note: Depression is counted as negative; exercise is positive.

  2. Choose the number of days you want to track.

  3. Answer 'Yes' or 'No' daily to record your progress.

  4. Collect coins and level up with each milestone.

  5. Flexible Tracking: Hit a blocker? Your progress is saved, and you can resume tracking anytime without losing your current level.

Seamless Connection, Instant Support

Chat Feature Tutorial

  1. Tap the 'Chat' button to open the chat interface.

  2. Enter desired tags in the search bar to find related chat rooms.

  3. Select a chat room from the list of tagged chats.

  4. Enter the chat room and engage in discussions.
